Welcome to our host Steve Chen!

Our emcee for the day will be Steve Chen. Steve is heavily involved in the DC and NYC tech meetup community. In DC, he co-founded DC Code & Coffee, hosted groups like NodeDC & DCJS, and now runs ReactDC and DC Product & Pastries. In NYC and beyond, he founded and operates as the Community Executive Officer of the Code & Coffee organization, a series of community-led meetups in 21 US cities and growing! He has even been recognized by Meetup for his work with NYC Code & Coffee. Previously he was the Head of Developer Relations at Openbase, and an early engineer at DC startups, Framebridge and Arcadia.

Thank you, Steve, for helping us bring back in-person events to DC and hosting Frontrunners!

Welcome to our co-host Casey Watts!

Casey is the founder of Happy and Effective, a company that does coaching, training, and consulting on organizational effectiveness and culture. Casey is the author of the applied psychology book Debugging Your Brain. Casey is a co-founder of the inclusive online community Empathy in Tech (join us!). Casey’s backgrounds are in psychology and software development, including work as an engineer, designer, product manager, ux designer, and engineering manager. Casey owns a different instrument in every color, and he can play Lady Gaga’s Bad Romance on all of them. Casey has one foot in Washington, DC and the other Baltimore, MD.
