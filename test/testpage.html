Keynote Speaker: Allison McMillan

Frontrunners is proud to announce Allison McMillan as our keynote speaker!

Allison McMillan is the VP of Engineering at QuotaPath. She has also been the Head of Engineering at Forem, the platform that powers Dev.to and Code Newbie, and previously, she was a Director of Engineering at GitHub, managing a variety of teams and products including Atom, Pull Requests, Repositories, and launching Codespaces. Formerly a software engineer, startup founder, Senior Program Director and community builder at the University of Michigan, and Managing Director of a national non-profit.
